Getting There

Indianola sits on the Kitsap Peninsula across Puget Sound from Seattle. Two easy ways in:

⛴️
Ferry via Bainbridge

Seattle → Bainbridge Island ferry (~35 min), then ~25 min drive north to Indianola.

⛴️
Ferry via Kingston

Edmonds → Kingston ferry (~30 min), then ~15 min drive south.

🚗
All the way around

Drive south through Tacoma + over the Narrows (~2 hr, no ferry). Good backup at odd hours.

🗺️ Open in Maps

Tip: ferries fill up on weekends — check sailing times & build in a buffer. [edit details]
